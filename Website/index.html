What is Tech-IT?

Tech-IT is an IT repair workshop set up by a former student of BCOT. The repairs are carried out by students on the computer science course as part of their education. The money gained from Tech-IT is then reinvested back into Tech-IT and the course itself to fund new resources for the students.

Tech-IT also saves it's customers money by charging extremely low prices compared to other competitors. We will repair any computing devices such as smartphones, laptops, desktops and tablets.

How Do I Book my Device into Tech-IT?

There are two ways to book your device into Tech-IT. First of all you can use our form which can be found here. Or alternatively you can give the Computer Science department a call on 01256 306663.
